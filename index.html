About

This app is a mobile equivalent for NASA web APOD service - https://apod.nasa.gov/apod/astropix.html. An APOD stands for Astronomy Picture Of the Day. It provides pictures in the field of astronomy with an explanation on what is on the picture. With this app you can create a list of your favourite apods, so you can keep all of them in within your reach and access them immediately. App also provides users a functionality of adding their own apods using phone camera.

The objective of the application is to create a society of astronomy enthusiasts and share astronomy all across the world.
